About Us

We are one of the experts in design, development and manufacturer of plunger and diaphragm type metering dosing pumps, Electromagnetic pumps and Triplex High Pressure pumps.

With over 23 years experience in manufacturing high quality and durable pumps, Acme pumps provides a wide range of metering and dosing pumps with advanced engineering techniques, which ensure reliability and accuracy.

Pumps are manufactured in accordance with API 675 standard to fulfill the growing demands of industrial user.

Our infrastructural facility makes us a one of the leading manufacturer of controlled volume pumps. In house facility for manufacturing, quality control and testing enable us to garner long list of satisfied clients.

We have the chemical feeding pumps from 0.5 LPH to 15000 LPH in plunger and diaphragm types. Well trained sales and technical support staffs are available to assist clients for all type of dosing applications.

We also supply dosing systems with pumps, pipe lines, mixing tanks, storage tanks, mixer, level gauges and control panels mounted on a common skid.

We are supporting customers with competitive price, timely feedback, specific technology backup, prompt delivery, reliable after-sale service, and enhancing the relations friendly with all customers.
